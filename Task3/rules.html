Word Search Puzzles

The rules of word search puzzles are as follows:

  1. Pick a word from the list next to the grid of letters
  2. Find the word in the grid and cross it out with a line
  3. The rules of the "crossing out" are as follows:
    • Pick a starting cell from the grid
    • Draw a line from the current cell to any neighbouring cell. Two cells are considered to be neighbours if they share an edge or a corner.
    • Repeat this process until you reach the last cell.
  4. If you take the letters in the order you crossed them out and this sequence matches the word you were searching for, it is considered that you've found the word.
